Mindfulness Practices to Help You Get Through this Season

Mindfulness is the ability to be fully present and aware where we are and what we’re doing, while not getting overwhelmed. While mindfulness is something we all naturally possess, it’s more readily available to us when we practice on a daily basis. Whenever you bring awareness to what you’re directly experiencing via your senses, or to your state of mind via your thoughts and emotions, you’re being mindful.
